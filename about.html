Who We Are

IKAMETI is a specialized residency-services team in Turkey. We exist because residency procedures are administratively complex, scattered and easy to get wrong — and one small mistake can cost you weeks or a rejection.

We turn that process into clear, practical steps: you always know what you need, when to submit it, and how to avoid common mistakes. We don't just file paperwork — we manage your residency case from the first conversation to the final decision and beyond.

🎯
