Overview

At Ikameti, we focus on the administrative complexity and lack of clarity in residency procedures.

We turn that process into practical steps so every client knows what they need, when to submit it, and how to avoid common mistakes. We do not just provide a service - we build an integrated residency request management system that starts with understanding the client's situation and ends with the decision and follow-up afterward.

🎯

Our Vision and Mission

To build a platform that becomes the first reference for anyone who wants to settle in Turkey, through a clear, fast, and scalable digital experience. To deliver reliable residency services that rely on clear steps, fast execution, and minimizing errors and rejections as much as possible.
